How much does it cost to own a website?

A website's cost depends entirely on the type of site you need. Since there are many different categories of websites, a few of the most common types are listed below:

  • 1. Information/Portfolio Website
  • 2. Blog and News Website
  • 3. Online Stores
  • 4. Online Listing Websites and Portals

How much does it cost to build an information or portfolio website?

Creating a business website depends on various factors. Costs are influenced by the site's aesthetics, the choice of programming languages and frameworks, the Content Management System (CMS), the technology platform, and the hosting provider . Furthermore, the development method and the experience and skill level of the professional building your site play a significant role in the final price .

Development methods include DIY/no-code builders, WordPress, low-code builders, custom development, AI-assisted coding, and AI app builders . Creating a website using DIY platforms like Wix or Squarespace is a simple, multi-step process: you choose a template, modify the content and sections using a visual editor, purchase a domain, and launch the site.

These solutions are ideal for boutique businesses and small service providers. For instance, Wix offers approximately 2,000 templates , allowing you to launch a business website much faster than other methods. Similarly, Squarespace enables the creation of aesthetically pleasing sites with built-in features to accept payments and sell products . However, keep in mind that these platforms are not as highly configurable as low-code builders or WordPress.

Low-code builders such as Webflow and Framer offer more features than no-code builders, though they require a higher level of design skill and basic technical knowledge.

You can build a more aesthetically pleasing website using low-code builders than you can with other platforms. One of their standout features is the ability to create elegant animations that can truly impress your visitors. These tools are highly suitable for all types of organizations-from small startups to large companies-looking to showcase their business information. Furthermore, like other DIY options, low-code builders include their own hosting and built-in Content Management Systems (CMS) .

WordPress , on the other hand, is the world's most popular CMS; the majority of business websites, news portals, personal blogs, and online stores run on this platform. WordPress offers numerous plugins and themes to extend its functionality. You can purchase a pre-made theme from an online marketplace and use Page Builder plugins like Elementor or Divi Builder to design pages from scratch.

To get started, you only need a basic WordPress tutorial, or you can hire a professional WordPress designer to build the site for you. Additionally, you can buy Elementor-compatible themes and easily modify the content and page sections using its intuitive visual builder .

If you want to create a contact form in WordPress , you will need to install a form plugin , which can be either a free or a paid version. Similarly, for SEO content optimization , you should choose a plugin such as Rank Math, Yoast SEO, or SEOPress . While these plugins offer free plans , you may need to upgrade to their paid plans for more advanced optimization of your blog or website.

When it comes to hosting a WordPress website , you will need to select a hosting provider, with shared hosting being a common choice for those starting out.

Building a custom website involves hiring a designer and a developer, selecting a hosting provider, and choosing a Content Management System (CMS) or database . If the site is dynamic , you must also decide on the appropriate programming languages and frameworks. The cost of development is significantly affected by the skills of the design and development team, their geographic location, and the specific technology stack they utilize.

Interestingly, the annual hosting cost for a custom brochure website is often lower than the recurring subscription fees charged by proprietary website builders.

A custom website grants the owner greater autonomy and control over their digital presence . Generally, the development of a static custom website costs less than that of a dynamic website, as the latter requires a Content Management System (CMS) or a database to store and manage web and blog content.

Building a simple website with one to five pages and a few custom features typically takes one to two weeks. However, by leveraging the power of AI tools , it is now possible to significantly reduce development timelines.

Before choosing custom development, you must consider your overall marketing budget, including ad campaigns on Google, Facebook, and Instagram , as well as email marketing, social media management , and other promotional activities .

It is essential to plan your marketing budget carefully before diving into website creation. You should also calculate the estimated break-even point for your investment and consider how a custom-built site will shape the way the public perceives your brand or business.
